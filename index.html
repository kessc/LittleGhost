Little Ghost

Pop Up Crayon Café

2022.

Your favourite pop up café is back! This year, you can find us at 110373 – 47 False Creek Edmonton, AB.

Location

From January 1st to April 30th, 2022, Little Ghost can be found in the heart of the city, at 110373 – 47 False Creek Edmonton. View the map below for directions.

Activities

Little Ghost provides a place to meet, work, laugh, and create, and features attractions such as table drawing and local art exhibitions.

Come in for a latte and a treat, and leave inspired, refreshed and delighted! We can’t wait to meet you and welcome you to the Little Ghost family.
